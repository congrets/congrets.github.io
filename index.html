About

Who am I?

My name is Alan Spektor and I am a sophmore at the Georgia Institute of Technology studying Computer Science and Computational Media. I'm interested in back-end design and video game development. My hobbies include puzzle solving, chess, and analyzing different forms of media.



Why am I interested in Computer Science?

I believe that the ability to actualize one's desires is the most important ability anyone can have. I have many ideas for contraptions, games, etc. that I believe knowing how to program would be essential to bringing them to life. As a result of this, I pursue my field because I want to have the ability to bring my ideas to the real world. I'd like for my creations to have a positive impact on future generations the way past creations have improved my life.



Where do I see myself in three to five years?

I see myself working as a software developer, being able to operate reliably with minimal oversight and contribute to improvements at my company. Leaading up to and during this time I will be improving my skills to qualify for a managerial position so I can oversee and manage a plethora of different projects, making sure each one is being developed efficiently and properly.
